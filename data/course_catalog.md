# AI Foundations Course Catalog

## Course Overview
AI Foundations introduces students to artificial intelligence, machine learning, generative AI, prompt engineering, responsible AI, and cloud-based AI services.

## Prerequisites
Students should understand basic programming concepts, APIs, and data formats such as JSON. Prior machine learning experience is helpful but not mandatory.

## Grading Policy
The final grade is based on weekly quizzes worth 20 percent, coding assignments worth 30 percent, a group project worth 30 percent, and a final presentation worth 20 percent.

## Attendance Requirement
Students must attend at least 75 percent of live sessions. Recorded sessions are available, but they do not fully replace live participation.

## Responsible AI Policy
Students must not submit AI-generated work without disclosure. Any AI tool usage must be documented in the assignment notes.

## Support
Students can ask questions in the learning portal. Faculty response time is usually within two working days.
